Welcome to the Palace of Versailles!

The Palace of Versailles is a former royal residence built by King Louis XIV. It is located about 12 miles west of Paris. Construstion of the Palace of Versailles started in 1634 and ended in 1762. The palace has 2300 rooms and is 3147 squared miles and is now a museum.
