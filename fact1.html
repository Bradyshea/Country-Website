Welcome to the Eiffel Tower!

The Eiffel Tower is a wrought-iron lattice tower on the Champ de Mars in Paris, France. It is named after Gustave Eiffel because his company designed and built the tower. It was constructed from 1887 to 1889, taking 2 years 2 months and 5 days and was the centerpice of the World's fair.
